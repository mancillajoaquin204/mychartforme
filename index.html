FAQ
How many times can I wash with a membership?

As long as your local ZIPS is open, Members enjoy up to 2 car washes per day, 7 days a week for as long as the membership is active!

What are the terms and conditions of my membership?

Click here for ZIPS Unlimited™ Terms of Use.

Can I use any location?

Yes, absolutely! Once you're a member, you gain access to any Zips Car Wash location that accepts Wash Club memberships, which includes a network of more than 260 car wash facilities across the U.S.

What if I want to upgrade my membership?

We’re happy to help you move on up in your membership. You can visit your local Zips Car Wash or contact us here to submit a request and we’ll get that changed to whichever package you like.

I got a new car, what do I do?

Don’t sweat it! Just visit your local Zips Car Wash and let an attendant know that you currently have a membership, but you got a new car and need to update your vehicle information to get a new RFID tag. They’ll take care of everything for you!

Can I use this membership on more than one vehicle?

Unfortunately, no-can-do. Each membership is tied to a specific car. The RFID stickers that we place on your windshield allow for that vehicle to pass through the car wash. We have to issue one for each vehicle because once removed, the tag is ruined and won’t scan properly.

Can I suspend my account?

No problem. If there is a reason why you’re unable to use your membership for a month or longer, we can cancel the billing so that you won’t be charged during that time.To learn more about this option, submit a request here.

How do I cancel?

All cancellation requests must be made 7 days prior to the anniversary billing date. Call (855) 947-7927. Our Call Center is open Monday through Friday, 8:00 am to 5:00 pm Central Time.
